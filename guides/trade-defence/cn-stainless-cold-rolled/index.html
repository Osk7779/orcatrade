EU Trade Defence Measure

Anti-dumping duty on stainless steel cold-rolled flat products from China: 25.3%

The headline rate

The country-wide rate for stainless steel cold-rolled flat products from China is 25.3% (named cooperating exporters as low as 24.4%), applied in addition to the MFN duty rate.

The legal basis

The duty is imposed by Reg. (EU) 2015/1429 (renewed). Also see ID, IN, TW for related stainless measures.

What products are covered

HS prefixes: 7219, 7220. Origin: China.

Worked example: €50,000 customs value

Imagine you are importing €50,000 of stainless steel cold-rolled flat products from China into PL. The MFN duty for the chapter is around 0.0%. With AD of 25.3% layered on top, the total customs duty rate is 25.3%.
