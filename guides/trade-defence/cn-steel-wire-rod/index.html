EU Trade Defence Measure

Anti-dumping duty on wire rod of iron or non-alloy steel from China: 17.9%

The headline rate

The country-wide rate for wire rod of iron or non-alloy steel from China is 17.9% (named cooperating exporters as low as 14.6%), applied in addition to the MFN duty rate.

The legal basis

The duty is imposed by Reg. (EU) 2022/619. Subject to safeguard quotas as well.

What products are covered

HS prefix: 7213. Origin: China.

Worked example: €50,000 customs value

Imagine you are importing €50,000 of wire rod of iron or non-alloy steel from China into PL. The MFN duty for the chapter is around 0.0%. With AD of 17.9% layered on top, the total customs duty rate is 17.9%.
