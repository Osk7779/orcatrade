What OrcaTrade Intelligence does.

Three core modules built for the realities of modern cross-border trade.

01

Factory Risk Scoring & Compliance Automation

Continuous monitoring of supplier risk signals — from audit history and certification status to geopolitical exposure and labour compliance flags. Automated scoring so you always know which factories need attention.

02

EUDR & CBAM Regulatory Tracking

Stay ahead of the EU Deforestation Regulation and Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism. Automated due diligence workflows, georeferenced data collection, and compliance status dashboards so your imports stay on the right side of EU law.

03

Supply Chain Disruption Forecasting

Early warning signals for port congestion, factory shutdowns, carrier capacity crunches, and macro trade disruptions. Forecast impact on your specific lanes and suppliers — not just headline news.
