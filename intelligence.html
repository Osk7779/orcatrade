How it works

One workflow, not three disconnected tools.

The workflow is simple: bring in the order, supplier, or shipment you are worried about, let OrcaTrade decide what matters, then act on the missing facts, risks, and next steps.

01
Bring In The Real Context
Start from a shipment, a supplier, or an import order. The platform works best when operations, sourcing, and compliance all point at the same real-world flow.
02
See What Applies
OrcaTrade shows which regulations apply, which factories look risky, which shipments need attention, and which facts are still missing before a safe decision can be made.
03
Move The Workflow Forward
Every result points to the next action: collect evidence, escalate a supplier, review a factory, or run the full compliance flow with article-level detail.
04
Work From One Exception Queue
Instead of inboxes and scattered notes, the important issues land in one place with the right urgency, owner, and operational context.
