The problem

Global trade runs on guesswork.

Most companies find out something is wrong when it's already too late to act.

⚠

Supplier blindness

You find out a factory is failing when your shipment doesn't arrive. By then, production delays, reorders, and penalties are already locked in.

⚖

Compliance chaos

EUDR, CBAM, CSDDD. Most brands don't know if they're compliant until they're fined. The regulatory landscape changes faster than any compliance team can track manually.

📡

No early warning

Port congestion, tariff changes, factory shutdowns. You hear about them weeks too late — after your freight is already booked and your customer is expecting delivery.
