Enterprise access, end-to-end reproducibility, in-app agent

Enterprise accessRBAC + SCIM + enforced-SSO
  • Six-role permission matrix (owner, admin, analyst, finance, compliance officer, viewer) with a fail-closed can() contract enforced server-side. Member invite/remove/role gated by ORG_MEMBERS_MANAGE; billing checkout and portal gated by BILLING_MANAGE.
  • SCIM 2.0 user provisioning at /api/scim/v2/* — your IdP (Okta, Entra ID) auto-provisions and deprovisions members with a per-org bearer token. Includes Groups → roles mapping (push a user into the finance group; their role changes accordingly), idempotent create, and ServiceProviderConfig discovery.
  • Enforced single sign-on per org — once an org has a complete OIDC config + enforceSso, magic-link sign-in for its claimed email domains is refused with a clear pointer to its SSO initiate URL.
  • Team admin UI in the app shell (/app/team) — list members with their roles, change roles, invite, remove; an owner-only SCIM/SSO panel mints and rotates the provisioning token.
ReproducibilityAs-of recompute, end to end
  • Every plan now carries a content-addressed snapshot (ds_…) of the volatile market data behind its numbers — FX rates, CBAM ETS price, every AD/CVD measure rate, plus the resolved MFN rate. Identical data ⇒ identical id, forever.
  • GET /api/plans/<id>/reproduce returns one of data-unchanged (numbers reproduce identically), data-drifted (itemises which values moved and recomputes the original landed total from the stored snapshot), drift-snapshot-unavailable, or no-snapshot-bound.
  • Reproducibility surfaced in the app — a panel on the plan detail view + a per-row badge (✓ reproducible / ◆ drifted) on the plans list, so the guarantee is visible where users live.
Pillar IVIn-app agent + operational cockpit
  • Streaming in-app agent chat (/app/chat) — the orchestrator at the centre of the authenticated product, with live tool-call activity and the personal-tools (memory, your saved plans) engaged via your session cookie.
  • Operations dashboard (/app/operations) — exposure heatmap and drift ledger across your saved plans, every figure deterministic.
  • First-run onboarding on /app/dashboard — guided three-step activation for brand-new accounts.
DocumentsCBAM + EUDR generators
  • Added CBAM Quarterly Report and EUDR Due Diligence Statement draft generators alongside the existing commercial invoice / packing list / proforma / certificate-of-origin set. Both pre-fill from a saved plan and clearly flag the domain data that only the installation or supplier can provide.
TrustAudit + security docs
  • Documented the tamper-evident audit trail: write-time hash chain over a personal-data-free projection, two verification endpoints (one stored-chain re-walk, one independently-verifiable export), and how GDPR Article 17 erasure coexists with chain integrity. See Trust & security.

Earlier ships are recorded in the apex plan log on GitHub. For security questions or DPA review, see /trust/.
