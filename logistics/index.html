Service tiers

Three ways to ship with us.

From transactional booking through full import management to compliance advisory.

Tier 1 · Transactional

Freight Forwarding Lite.

Resold capacity from established forwarders (DSV, Kuehne+Nagel, Röhlig Suus, Raben). We handle booking, documentation, and customer communication — you ship without owning the forwarder relationship.

  • Booking with vetted forwarder partners
  • Bill of Lading / Airway Bill issuance
  • In-transit tracking with proactive ETA updates
  • Document handover to your broker or ours

Indicative pricing: 8–15% margin on freight cost · service fee €150–€500 / shipment

Tier 2 · Flagship

Full Import Management.

The end-to-end run, where the Hong Kong presence and supplier relationships do the work. From supplier vetting to last-mile, we run the shipment and you stay informed — not buried in email chains.

  • Supplier vetting and price negotiation
  • Quality inspection coordination (QIMA, AsiaInspection, or our inspectors)
  • Chinese export documentation and freight booking
  • EU customs clearance via licensed broker · VAT and duty
  • Last-mile delivery coordination and Proof of Delivery

Indicative pricing: €800–€2,500 / shipment OR 3–7% of cargo value · pass-through costs

Tier 3 · Advisory

Compliance & Advisory.

Direct line into OrcaTrade Intelligence's EU compliance engine: CBAM, CE marking, REACH, EUDR, product safety directives, sustainability reporting. Audit packages, not retainers.

  • CBAM applicability, exposure, and certificate planning
  • CE marking and EU product safety directive scoping
  • REACH and substance restriction reviews
  • EUDR readiness — geolocation, due diligence, declarations
  • Sustainability and ESG reporting prep

Indicative pricing: advisory hours OR audit packages €500–€3,000

How we work

A codified shipment playbook.

Every shipment runs the same flow — so the same problems get caught at the same step, every time, regardless of who is on shift.

  1. 01

    Supplier confirmation & contract

    Final terms, payment milestones, Incoterm, target ship date.

  2. 02

    Pre-shipment booking

    Capacity reserved with the right partner forwarder for the route and mode.

  3. 03

    Pre-shipment inspection

    If scoped — coordinated with QIMA, AsiaInspection, or our inspectors.

  4. 04

    Export documentation

    Commercial invoice, packing list, CO, fumigation certificate where required.

  5. 05

    BL / AWB issued · in-transit monitoring

    Proactive ETA updates, route-pressure alerts from OrcaTrade Intelligence.

  6. 06

    EU customs clearance

    Polish licensed broker partner. HS code, duty, VAT, declaration filed.

  7. 07

    Last-mile delivery & POD

    Final invoice, reconciliation, and a clean handover into your warehouse.

Every shipment is logged step-by-step in a shared workspace. Your team plugs in at any point without losing context — same view as ours.
