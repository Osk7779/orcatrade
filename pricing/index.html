FAQ

Common questions before you commit.

Can I switch tiers later?

Yes — upgrade or downgrade any time. Annual subscribers get prorated credit on upgrades. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

Is the Free tier really free forever?

Yes. The Free tier is permanent and doesn't require a payment method. It's enough to evaluate the platform — but not enough to run a real import operation on. Free includes 20 agent queries, 10 supplier views, 5 documents, and 5 HS lookups per month, plus unlimited free CBAM analyses.

Why is annual two months free instead of a flat discount?

Annual subscribers churn at roughly one-third the rate of monthly. The two-months-free framing is more honest than a percentage and easier to compare against. You pay 10 months, get 12.

How does VAT work for Polish, German, and other EU customers?

Stripe Tax handles EU VAT automatically. Polish customers see prices including 23% VAT, German customers 19%, and so on. Reverse-charge applies to valid B2B VAT IDs across the EU.

What's the difference between Growth and Scale?

Growth is built for SMEs running 5–50 shipments per year — most customers belong here. Scale unlocks unlimited agent queries, custom agent training on your supplier data, 20 seats, full API access, and a dedicated account manager. Move to Scale once your team is large enough to need API integration or custom training.

Do you require per-user pricing?

No — core tiers include reasonable seat counts (Starter 1, Growth 5, Scale 20). Additional seats above the included count are charged per seat, but seat count alone never forces a tier upgrade.

How does the Compliance Agent integrate with my existing workflow?

The Compliance Agent runs inside the dashboard and via API. Drop in product details and origin; it returns HS code (with confidence score), expected duty and VAT, applicable regulations, required documents, and a checklist with cost and timeline estimates. Every quote routes to a human for final approval initially, automating further only after supervised quote volume.

Can I see the platform before paying?

Yes — run a free CBAM analysis right now without signing up. The Free tier and 14-day trial cover everything else.

What if I'm Enterprise but don't fit the standard SLA?

Enterprise pricing is bespoke. We tailor SLAs, ERP integrations, white-label deployment, and named-AM coverage to whatever your operation needs. Talk to sales — we'll size it together.
