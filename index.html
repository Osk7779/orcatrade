How we work

A simple 6-step sourcing flow.

Whether you are placing your first order from Asia or adding a new product line, our process stays the same: clear briefs, transparent communication, documented decisions.

  1. 01

    Discovery & product brief

    We align on your product specs, target landed cost, volume and timelines. If you're early, we help translate ideas into factory-ready requirements.

  2. 02

    Factory search & shortlist

    We reach out to targeted manufacturers, filter for capability & reliability, and return a curated shortlist with indicative pricing and MOQs.

  3. 03

    Sampling & fine-tuning

    Samples are produced, shipped and evaluated together with you. We refine specs, finishes and packaging to lock in the golden sample.

  4. 04

    Order placement & production

    Once you approve, we confirm POs, payment terms and production slots. You receive a clear timeline from deposit to shipment window.

  5. 05

    Quality control & approvals

    Inspections are booked according to risk level and product type. Only after passing QC do we sign off on shipment.

  6. 06

    Logistics & after-shipment care

    We liaise with forwarders, align documents and track the shipment. If there are post-arrival issues, we help address them with the factory.

Every project runs in shared channels & summary documents, so your team can plug in at any time without losing context.
