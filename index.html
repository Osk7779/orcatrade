Sourcing & procurement

Supplier scouting with real filters.

We identify, vet and negotiate with manufacturers that can truly deliver on spec, volume and compliance – not just quote aggressively.

  • Supplier mapping across China & selected Asian hubs
  • Audit of certifications, capacity and export track record
  • Sample coordination and benchmarking
  • Price & payment term negotiation on your behalf
Quality & compliance

From drawings to cartons – under control.

We implement structured quality gates so issues are caught before they leave the factory floor – not at your warehouse dock.

  • Spec sheets and golden sample definition
  • On-site inspections via accredited QC partners
  • Photo & video reporting from the production line
  • Packaging, labelling & documentation checks
Logistics coordination

We sit between factory, forwarder & finance.

You keep one contact – we orchestrate the rest. From EXW to FOB or DAP, we sync timelines, documents and stakeholders.

  • Coordination with your freight forwarder or our network
  • Consolidation across multiple factories
  • Commercial invoice & packing list validation
  • Support on Incoterms, HS codes & customs basics
How we work

A simple 6-step sourcing flow.

Whether you are placing your first order from Asia or adding a new product line, our process stays the same: clear briefs, transparent communication, documented decisions.

  1. 01

    Discovery & product brief

    We turn your brief into factory-ready requirements.

  2. 02

    Factory search & shortlist

    A curated shortlist with pricing, capability and MOQs.

  3. 03

    Sampling & fine-tuning

    We refine until the golden sample is locked.

  4. 04

    Order placement & production

    POs confirmed with a clear deposit-to-shipment timeline.

  5. 05

    Quality control & approvals

    Shipment only proceeds after passing inspection.

  6. 06

    Logistics & after-shipment care

    We track, document and resolve any post-arrival issues.

Every project runs in shared channels & summary documents, so your team can plug in at any time without losing context.
