FAQ

Is "lifetime" actually for life?

Yes — as long as your Growth subscription stays continuously active. Cancel for two consecutive months and the founder rate retires. We'll honour it on every renewal otherwise, including future price rises on the standard Growth tier.

What if you go bust before "lifetime" matters?

Then OrcaTrade doesn't bill you for what we can't deliver. Stripe Checkout is the system of record; if we shut down, your card stops being charged. You won't be locked into anything.

Why only ten spots?

Honestly — because more than ten founder-Slack conversations stops being a feedback loop and starts being a help desk. Ten is the number that lets us actually respond same-day and ship against what you say. Past ten, we open Pro and Scale at full price and the platform stops being shaped by individual customers.

Can I apply if I'm only doing one shipment a quarter?

Yes — but Growth is sized for 5–50 shipments / year. If you're at one a quarter, Starter (€99) is probably better, and we'll happily point you there instead. The Founding 10 is specifically for importers whose volume can stress-test the calculators.

Can my company afford to be on the homepage?

If you'd rather not be named publicly, we'll list "Founding 10 — confidential" and skip the quote. The 50% off and Slack channel still apply. We won't pressure you on naming.
