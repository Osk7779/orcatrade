Founder

Oskar Klepuszewski
Founder & CFO · OrcaTrade Holding

Built OrcaTrade after watching Polish SMEs lose 2–5% of every Asian shipment to FX margin, opaque freight rates, and compliance gaps that no incumbent platform translated into action. The product emerged from a calculator-grounded approach: every recommendation traces back to numbers an importer can audit and a regulation an importer can cite.

What we build

A trilingual import workspace combining real-time landed-cost calculators (sourcing, routing, customs, warehouse, FX, working capital), five specialised AI agents, an EU compliance engine covering CBAM/EUDR/REACH/CE/AD-CVD, plus saved-plan history and Stripe-billed subscription tiers. Customers self-serve through the wizard at /start/; the agents and compliance overlays surface on every page.

Editorial stance

OrcaTrade is built around calibrated trust over breadth. We don't index the whole of Alibaba. We don't promise every supplier on every continent. We do tell importers exactly what their landed cost will be, where the regulatory landmines are, and which Asian origin saves them the most. Numbers always trace to calculators; recommendations always trace to regulations.
