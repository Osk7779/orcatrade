Impact programs

Three programs. One ocean.

Focused initiatives where foundation contributions go to work.

Program 01

Orca Habitat Protection

Partnering with marine biologists and conservation NGOs to protect critical orca habitats along key trade shipping corridors. Funding acoustic monitoring, vessel speed reduction programmes, and habitat mapping in partnership with regional authorities.

Program 02

Marine Clean-Up Network

Supporting community-led shoreline and ocean clean-up operations across Asia-Pacific and European coastal zones. Each program is tracked with before/after impact data and co-funded by client contributions tied to their sourcing volumes.

Program 03

Ocean Education & Advocacy

Educational programs for coastal communities, schools, and industry stakeholders on the link between global commerce and ocean health. Building the next generation of advocates who understand that trade and conservation are not opposites.

Foundation leadership

The person behind the Foundation.

Sir Timotheus Carrington

OrcaTrade Foundation Lead

A descendant of the historic Aristorcacry lineage, Sir Timotheus Carrington brings an old-money philanthropic ethos to the OrcaTrade Foundation. He leads long-horizon programs for orca protection and marine habitat restoration, combining legacy stewardship with modern partnerships, conservation funding and high-visibility advocacy to create measurable impact across ocean ecosystems.

Sir Timotheus brings a patient, legacy-first approach to conservation — one that favours durable partnerships and measurable outcomes over headline philanthropy. Under his leadership, the Foundation operates with the same discipline and transparency that OrcaTrade Holding applies to every sourcing programme.
