How it works

No PDF tools. No Word documents. No customs delays.

All documents are generated server-side as printable HTML. Print to PDF from any browser.

01
Fill the form
Multi-section form with auto-saved draft state. Add or remove line items, paste banking details, declare country of origin per line.
02
Generate
We render the document server-side. No browser quirks, no font fallbacks. Customs-clean formatting, every time.
03

Use your browser's print dialog → "Save as PDF". Ready for the broker, the customer, or the bank.
04
Re-use the data
Your exporter / consignee / shipment details are saved locally. The next document re-uses them — fill once, print many.
