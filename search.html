What OrcaTrade Search does.

Purpose-built for importers who need to find the right factory fast.

01

Search by Category, Country & MOQ

Granular search filters let you narrow by product type, manufacturing country, minimum order quantity, export certifications, and production capacity — so your shortlist starts with factories that already fit your requirements.

02

Verified Manufacturer Profiles

Every factory in OrcaTrade Search has been reviewed for export track record, certifications, and capacity. Profiles include audit status, key product lines, certifications, photos, and verified contact details — no cold outreach to unvetted suppliers.

03

Direct Sourcing Pipeline Integration

Shortlisted factories can be handed directly into an OrcaTrade Sourcing engagement — or exported to your own procurement workflow. Search feeds directly into action, not into another spreadsheet.
