Cosmetics from India — Responsible Person, CPNP notification, REACH SVHC + GSP standard

cosmeticsIN→DEGSP + Cosmetics + REACH

An importer bringing a face-care line from a Mumbai supplier, €30,000 per shipment. Cosmetics chapter 33 triggers Cosmetics Regulation 1223/2009: every product needs an EU Responsible Person, a Product Information File, and CPNP notification before market placement. REACH applies for SVHC checks. India's GSP standard provides ~30% duty reduction (no zero-rate). The compliance overlay alone often delays the first shipment 3–6 months.
