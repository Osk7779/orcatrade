# OrcaTrade · Letter of Engagement (LoE) — Templates

> One-page commercial-alignment documents for partner relationships. Send within 48 hours of a verbal "yes" on a partnership call. **Bilingual EN+PL** — pick the side the partner prefers; both versions are intentionally identical in legal content.
>
> The LoE is *not* a final contract. A bilingual contract from a Polish lawyer follows the LoE once countersigned. The LoE is the "we agree on these commercial terms" document.
>
> **Version:** 1.0 · 2026-05-07
> **Owner:** Oskar (commercial sign-off) · Arman (sending and follow-up)

---

## How to use

1. Verbal alignment from the partnership call (use the structure in [partner-outreach-scripts.md](./partner-outreach-scripts.md))
2. Pick the right template below — Forwarder, Customs Agency, Inspection
3. Fill in the bracketed fields `[like this]`
4. Send via DocuSign or signed PDF
5. Once countersigned, hand over to legal counsel for the bilingual full contract
6. Log in the partner-tracking sheet (schema in `partner-outreach-scripts.md`)

## Template A · Freight Forwarder Partnership

### EN — English version

```
LETTER OF ENGAGEMENT — FREIGHT FORWARDING PARTNERSHIP

Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]

Between:

  OrcaTrade Holding sp. z o.o.
  [registered address — Warsaw]
  KRS: [number] · NIP: [VAT number]
  Represented by: Oskar Klepuszewski, Co-Founder & CFO
  Email: oskar@orcatrade.pl

  ("OrcaTrade")

and:

  [Partner legal name]
  [Partner registered address]
  [Registration number / KRS]
  Represented by: [Name, Title]
  Email: [contact]

  ("Partner")

1. Subject

OrcaTrade and Partner agree to a non-exclusive freight-forwarding referral
relationship. OrcaTrade will refer customer shipments to Partner for booking,
movement, and document handover under Partner's existing forwarder licences.
OrcaTrade remains responsible for customer-facing communication.

2. Scope

  • Modes covered: [sea FCL · sea LCL · air · China–Europe rail]
  • Origin coverage: [China · Vietnam · India · other Asia hubs]
  • Destination coverage: [Poland · Germany · CEE · EU-wide via Polish customs]
  • Excluded: dangerous goods of Class [n] · refrigerated cargo · oversized
    project cargo (unless agreed in writing per shipment)

3. Volume forecast (non-binding)

  Year 1, by quarter: Q1 [n] shipments · Q2 [n] · Q3 [n] · Q4 [n]
  Year 2: ramp to [n] shipments quarterly
  No minimum-volume commitment by either party in Year 1.

4. Commercial terms

  • Partner offers OrcaTrade their published rate card minus [n]% volume discount
    on shipments above the Year-1 ramp threshold.
  • OrcaTrade adds its own service fee (€150–€500 per shipment) on top of the
    Partner rate. The mark-up is OrcaTrade's revenue and not shared.
  • Pass-through costs (THC, ISPS, customs disbursement, demurrage, detention)
    remain on the customer invoice with no OrcaTrade mark-up.

5. Operations & SLAs

  • Booking turnaround: [n] business hours from quote acceptance
  • In-transit visibility: Partner provides API or daily-emailed status updates
  • Document delivery: BL/AWB to OrcaTrade portal within [n] business hours
    of issuance
  • Named day-to-day contacts on both sides

6. Liability & insurance

  • Partner carries cargo and professional-liability insurance customary for
    the freight-forwarding industry.
  • Partner liability for any single claim is capped at 2 SDR per kilogramme
    (sea/road) or 19 SDR per kilogramme (air), per the Hague-Visby Rules and
    Montreal Convention respectively, unless cargo insurance is separately
    procured.
  • OrcaTrade is not liable for physical-cargo damage or loss handled by
    Partner.

7. Term & termination

  • Initial term: 12 months, automatically renewing for successive 12-month
    periods.
  • Either party may terminate on 30 days' written notice without cause.
  • Existing in-flight shipments at termination are completed under this LoE.

8. Confidentiality

  • Each party keeps confidential the other's customer lists, rate cards,
    and operational data, except where disclosure is required by law.
  • Confidentiality survives termination by 24 months.

9. Co-marketing (optional)

  • Either party may, with prior written consent, use the other's name and
    logo in marketing materials and case studies.
  • No exclusivity in any direction.

10. Path forward

  • This LoE is a commercial-alignment document. Within 30 days of
    countersignature, the parties will exchange a full bilingual contract
    drafted by Polish counsel.
  • Operational onboarding (API credentials, named contacts, system tests)
    will run in parallel.

11. Signatories

  For OrcaTrade Holding sp. z o.o.:

    Name:        Oskar Klepuszewski
    Title:       Co-Founder & CFO
    Signature:   _______________________
    Date:        ___________

  For [Partner]:

    Name:        [Name]
    Title:       [Title]
    Signature:   _______________________
    Date:        ___________
```

## Template B · Customs Agency Partnership

### EN — English version

```
LETTER OF ENGAGEMENT — CUSTOMS AGENCY PARTNERSHIP

Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]

Between:

  OrcaTrade Holding sp. z o.o.  [details as above]

and:

  [Customs Agency legal name]
  [Address] · KRS: [number] · NIP: [number]
  AEO status: [yes / no — number]
  Represented by: [Name, Title]

  ("Agency")

1. Subject

OrcaTrade refers EU customs declarations to Agency for filing on behalf of
OrcaTrade's customers. Agency files declarations under its own customs-broker
licence; OrcaTrade prepares and feeds the data. The relationship is
non-exclusive.

2. Scope of Agency services

  • Import declarations into Poland (and onward EU 27 via transit T1 where
    applicable)
  • VAT representation for non-EU clients (subject to separate sub-agreement
    if scoped)
  • Duty and VAT calculation, including CBAM-related amounts where applicable
  • EUDR Due Diligence Statement reference verification at customs release
  • Advisory hours on AEO, customs warehousing, inward processing relief
  • Excluded: export declarations from EU origin · transit only · maritime
    customs operations

3. Volume forecast

  Year 1: [n] declarations per month, ramping to [n]
  Year 2: [n] declarations per month
  No minimum volume commitment.

4. Commercial terms

  • Per-declaration fee: PLN [n] / EUR [n] (sliding scale by volume tier)
  • Volume tiers:
      0–20 declarations / month: PLN [n] each
      21–50 declarations / month: PLN [n] each
      51+ declarations / month: PLN [n] each
  • Advisory hours billed at PLN [n] / hour, weekly retainer optional
  • Disbursements (duty, VAT, customs handling fees) passed through at cost
    plus [n]% handling fee

5. Data flow & integration

  • OrcaTrade provides structured pre-clearance data (HS code, origin, value,
    incoterms, supporting documents) via [SFTP / API / email]
  • Agency files declarations within [n] business hours of complete data
  • Agency provides clearance reference and SAD (or electronic equivalent)
    back to OrcaTrade portal
  • Both parties retain decision-making authority on declaration content;
    Agency final-checks and signs.

6. Liability

  • Agency carries customs-broker professional indemnity insurance per Polish
    market standard.
  • Liability for declaration errors caused by Agency: capped at PLN [n] per
    declaration or [n]× the per-declaration fee, whichever is higher.
  • OrcaTrade is liable for the accuracy of the data provided by OrcaTrade
    or by OrcaTrade's customer.
  • For penalty exposure to the customer (CBAM Art. 26, EUDR Art. 25, REACH
    Art. 126, CE national penalties), each party is responsible for the
    portion attributable to its own role and data.

7. Term & termination

  • Initial term: 12 months, automatically renewing.
  • 30-day written notice termination by either party without cause.
  • In-flight declarations at termination are completed under this LoE.

8. Confidentiality

  Standard mutual NDA terms (24-month survival).

9. Co-branding

  • OrcaTrade may describe Agency as "our Polish customs partner" in
    customer-facing materials with prior written consent.
  • Agency may, with consent, list OrcaTrade as a referral source.

10. Path forward

  • Full bilingual contract within 30 days of countersignature.

11. Signatories

  [As above format]
```

## Notes for use

- All bracketed `[n]` values must be filled before sending. Do not send a
  template with unresolved placeholders.
- Polish counterparties typically prefer the PL version for signature and
  the EN version as a courtesy translation. Inspection partners (mostly in
  China / Vietnam / India) typically sign the EN version.
- Currency: keep Polish customs-agency contracts in PLN; freight forwarders
  and inspection partners typically EUR or USD.
- For cross-border parties, add a governing-law clause to the full contract
  (PL law for forwarders / customs agencies; English law optional for
  inspection partners depending on their preference).
- All LoE templates produce a single PDF page when typeset normally;
  expand only the legal-entity, scope, and commercial sections for the
  full contract.
