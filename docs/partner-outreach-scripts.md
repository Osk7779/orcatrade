# OrcaTrade · Partner Outreach Scripts

> Cold and warm outreach templates for the three Logistics-tier partner categories: freight forwarders, Polish customs agencies, and Asia inspection partners. Designed for direct execution by the founding team.
>
> **Use principle:** keep the first message short, specific, and asymmetrically valuable. We bring distribution to them — that's the lead.

---

## Workflow

1. Identify 5–10 targets per category from the lists below.
2. Send the cold email (template A). Follow up after 5 business days with template B.
3. If accepted to a call, run the partnership-call structure at the bottom.
4. Track in a simple sheet: name · contact · status · last touch · next action.

### What to bring to the partner call

- Forecast: cargo volume by mode (sea FCL/LCL, air, rail), origin port mix, destination port mix.
- Customer profile snapshot: SME, €50k–€2M annual, low complexity per shipment.
- Margin discussion: target 8–15% on freight cost as our lite-tier markup; willing to take a smaller cut on volume commitments.
- Operational requirements: API or EDI for booking + tracking; standard documentation pack; named day-to-day contact.
- Deal structure preference: non-exclusive, mode-specific (one preferred sea + one preferred air), revisit quarterly.

## Category 2 · Polish customs agency

**Why this matters:** OrcaTrade is **not a licensed customs agent** in the EU. Logistics Tier 2 (Full Import Management) and the Compliance & Advisory tier require a Polish *agencja celna* partner that can either white-label or operate as a subcontractor.

### Target profile

- Small-to-mid Polish customs agency (5–30 employees)
- AEO-certified preferred (Authorized Economic Operator)
- Strong on import-side declarations, VAT representation
- Willing to accept structured digital workflow (we feed clean data; they file)
- Willing to white-label or co-brand initially

### Discovery channels

- KIDP (Krajowa Izba Doradców Podatkowych) — Polish chamber of tax advisers
- KSOC (Krajowe Stowarzyszenie Osób Posiadających Uprawnienia Eksperta Celnego) — customs experts association
- Direct search on KRS for agencies with PKD 5229A and AEO status
- Customs brokers near Małaszewicze (rail gateway), Gdańsk (sea gateway), Warszawa (air via Okęcie + Lublin Airport)

## Partnership-call structure (for any partner type)

**Total: 30 minutes.**

1. **0–3 min · Frame.** Who we are, what we're building, why we're talking.
2. **3–10 min · Volume + flow.** What our deal flow looks like; numbers and lane mix; SLA expectations.
3. **10–20 min · Their side.** What they offer, pricing model, integration capability, pain points with similar customers.
4. **20–25 min · Deal structure.** Non-exclusive vs exclusive; pricing; volume thresholds; integration path; named contact.
5. **25–30 min · Next steps.** Either a follow-up call with their commercial lead, or a draft Letter of Engagement we send within 48 hours.

---

## Letter of Engagement (LoE) — outline

When a partner says yes verbally, send a one-page LoE within 48 hours.

- **Parties.** Names, addresses, signatories.
- **Scope.** What they do, what we do, what's out of scope.
- **Volume forecast.** Year-1 quarterly target, no minimum commitment.
- **Pricing.** Their rate card, our markup ceiling (where relevant), volume tiers.
- **Service levels.** Response times, working days, escalation path.
- **Liability.** Who carries what insurance; cap on liability.
- **Term.** 12-month rolling, 30-day termination notice on either side.
- **Confidentiality.** Mutual NDA referenced or attached.
- **Co-marketing.** Optional — logo usage, case studies, joint content.
- **Signing.** DocuSign or e-signature preferred.

> A real bilingual contract from a Polish lawyer comes after the LoE is countersigned. The LoE is the commercial alignment document; the contract is the legal one.
