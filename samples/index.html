How it works

Five steps · ~10 business days · one parcel.

01
Submit the request
Tell us which suppliers, what samples, target sample weight, your EU delivery address. We send pickup instructions to each supplier.
02
Suppliers ship to our HK office
Suppliers post samples to our Hong Kong consolidation address. We log every parcel on receipt with weight, dimensions, and date.
03
Photo-log + repack
Yiu's team photographs every item against a numbered grid, repackages into one parcel with the lightest viable filler. The photo log goes to you for sign-off before dispatch.
04
Final quote & dispatch
Final shipping quote based on real consolidated weight. On approval we ship via standard courier (DHL Economy / FedEx Economy) or express upgrade. Tracking link emailed.
05
Customs cleared as samples
EU clearance via our Polish broker partner. Samples typically declared at zero or nominal value (under €22) — usually duty- and VAT-free if properly marked "samples — no commercial value".

Why the HK consolidation works

Most SMEs underestimate the wasted spend.

Five separate parcels
Five suppliers each shipping a 0.5kg sample DHL Express to Europe directly: ~€45–€60 per parcel × 5 = €225–€300, plus customs fees on each entry.
One consolidated parcel
Same five samples through our HK office: €115 consolidation + €40 shipping (sub-5kg band) = €155 total. Saves €70–€145 plus 5 customs entries → 1.
Visibility you didn't have before
